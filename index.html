ABOUT

This is our humble history .

  • 2017-2018

    Our Humble Beginnings

    It all started when, in the fourth year of our Aeroespacial Engineering grade, a teacher suggested us about making a proyect. At first it looked like another average proyect, but it became quickly an amazing one!

  • March 2018

    An Agency is Born

    As soon as we finished our degree, all of us talked about the possibility of making that proyect happen, because we were amazed of the high looks it had.

  • December 2019

    Our First Take Off

    After nearly half a year we got all the sponsors and permissions, so we began to buy and organise everything we needed. After a pair of months, we got our first call! It was to take part on the huge weeding Clara & José were preparing. On March 21st our first official flight took off, and that was only the beginning!

  • July 2020

    Looking For An Expansion

    Actually we are performing almost daily flights in Leon, with several options available. But we also offer a lot of alternative activities for those days where the conditions aren’t the best. Furthermore, we are looking to expand our company and start offering our services in more and more places. We need some help, so if you want to become a partner of our company, check down below and contact us, neither of us will regret it!

GUIDED TOUR

Fly over León and be able to see the most emblematic areas of the city from the heights.

    MORNING SHIFT

Go to LE-508, km 1, Villaquilambre and have a complimentary lunch a 11.30 am.

Departure: 12.00 am.

Arrival: 01.00 pm.

    AFTERNOON SHIFT

Go to LE-508, km 1, Villaquilambre and have a complimentary snack at 18.00 am.

Departure: 6.00 pm.

Arrival: 7.00 pm.

    PRICES

  • Adults: 140€
  • Kids(6-14 years): 100€

MEDULAS

    AFTERNOON SHIFT
